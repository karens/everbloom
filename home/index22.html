Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Aug 15 2009 6:00 AM - Sat, Aug 15 2009 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Evergreen

Flickr photos from the August 15, 2009 album.

Hot temps, brisk southwest winds and sunny conditions met 18 teams at the 7th stop on the Strike King EverBloom Tournament Trail at Lake Evergreen. Rod Wallace and Pat Welch took home first place with a 5 bass limit weighing a very respectable 14.26 pounds. They flipped and pitched and caught their bass on an unnamed tube. Great job guys. In second were veteran anglers AJ Bailey and Scott Bree with a 5 bass limit weighing 12.57 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Jul 25 2009 5:00 AM - Sat, Jul 25 2009 1:00 PM
Location
Lake Bloomington

Flickr photos from the July 25, 2009 album.

The Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail visited Lake Bloomington on July 25 greeted by a cold front and wind, however the lake produced great fishing and 54 keepers were brought to the scales weighing 130.50 pounds. WOW! Starting at 5:15 am jackets were a must but temps later in the day rose to near 80 and fishing was even hotter. This lake is no longer Lake Doomington. The team of Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris bested the 20 boat field with 4 bass weighing 14.07 anchored by a 5.36 pound largemouth. They fished docks and grass. We would imagine a Zoom Brush Hog played here as well.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Jun 27 2009 5:00 AM - Sat, Jun 27 2009 1:00 PM
Location
Lake Evergreen

Flickr photos from the June 27, 2009 album.

Saturday June 27 was an exceptional fishing day for Terry Brown and Mike Blake at the Strike King EverBloom Tournament on Evergreen Lake. They had a 5 bass limit that weighed 19.06 pounds anchored by a giant 6.13 pounder fishing points and ledges on the main basin of Evergreen Lake with jigs and plastics. The team caught 14 keeper fish during the day and had their limit by 7:30 am. In second place was super steady Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris with 5 bass weighing 13.53 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, May 30 2009 5:00 AM - Sat, May 30 2009 1:00 PM
Location
Lake Evergreen

Flickr photos from the May 30, 2009 album.

The Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail completed the last event Saturday and the conditions were perfect. Warm temps, light winds, an earlier start time, 5:00 am and 15 boats looking for both spawning and post spawn bass. 2008 Team of the Year Kenny Petersen and Fred Myers had a great day with 5 bass weighing 13.38 pounds to take the victory. Their day started out tough as they reported losing 5 keepers before 8:30 am. They fished mid lake areas and caught their fish on a Mr. P, a chatterbiat designed and made by Petersen, a Zoom Horny Toad and a Reaction Innovations Sweet Beaver.
