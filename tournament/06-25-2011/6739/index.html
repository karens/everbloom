Long and Devine Win Rapala EverBloom Qualifier

Ron Long and Al Devine were the last boat out at take out but at the end of the day weighed the most fish to take the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail Qualifier at Lake Evergreen June 25th. They weighed 5 bass at 15.06 pounds. In second place were Jamie Maisenmacher and Brad Norris with 5 bass weighing 12.83 pounds. In third were Brian Bounds and Jon Hodge with 11.24 pounds. Fred Myers had Sam Leman Big Bass for the event with a 5.06 pounder.

The Sam Leman Heavyweight Leaders are Aaron Therian and Mike Therian with 40.52 pounds. In second are Ryan Robinson and Dave Whalen with 34.20 pounds. Third is Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 33.39 pounds.

Next tournament is at Lake Bloomington July 30. We will take off at 5:30 am.
