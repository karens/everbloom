Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Mike Blake on Sun, 08/16/2009 - 6:44pm
Date: 
Saturday, August 15, 2009 - 6:00am to 2:00pm
Location: 
Lake Evergreen
Source: Flickr

Hot temps, brisk southwest winds and sunny conditions met 18 teams at the 7th stop on the Strike King EverBloom Tournament Trail at Lake Evergreen.

Rod Wallace and Pat Welch took home first place with a 5 bass limit weighing a very respectable 14.26 pounds. They flipped and pitched and caught their bass on an unnamed tube. Great job guys.

In second were veteran anglers AJ Bailey and Scott Bree with a 5 bass limit weighing 12.57 pounds. They too flipped and pitched to catch their bass.

In third place were Larry Russell and Randy Ferguson with two bass weighing 8.21 lbs anchored by the Sam Leman Big Bass of 5.15 pounds.

Rounding out the top 5 were Brad Norris and Jamie Maisenbacher with 6.86 pounds and Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 6.03 pounds.

Brown and Blake lead the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year standings with 52.41 pounds followed by Brad Norris and Jamie Maisenbacher with 46.04 and Dusty Whitacre and Bryan Estes with 45.77 pounds respectively.

Brown and Blake also have the Sam Leman big bass for the year at 6.13 pounds.

Our next stop will be the last regularly scheduled event at Lake Bloomington on August 29. We will be fishing from 6 am to 2 pm that day.
