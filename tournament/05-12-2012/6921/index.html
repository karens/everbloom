Hakes and Hakes Win at Evergreen!

Cloudy skies, very little wind and fish in all stages of the spawn greeted competitors at Lake Evergreen May 12th. What set up as perfect conditions and high expectations turned just the opposite with only 26 total bass brought to the scales.

Brothers Randy and Rob Hakes took home first at the second Sam Leman EverBloom event with 4 fish weighing 10.53 pounds. They are also leading the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year standings after two events with 20.89 pounds.

In second place was Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 3 fish weighing 8.15 pounds. Andy Wegner and Dusty Whitacre got third with 4 fish weighing 8.02 pounds. They took home two Duckett Microguide Rods for their finish. Fourth place was Jerry Martoglio and Larry Andres with 3 fish weighing 7.99 pounds. Fifth place was taken by Scott Punke and Ray Moushon with 5.93 pounds. They took home the Zoom Top to Bottom packages for fifth. Punke and Moushon also had big bass of 3.85 pounds and won cash plus 2 Advantage Bait Company Spinnerbaits for big fish.

The next event will be on Lake Bloomington May 26th. Remember to drop your money by Wired2Fish to guarantee your spot in that tournament.
