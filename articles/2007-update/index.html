2007 Update

By Mike Blake, 27 March, 2007

Fellow Ever-Bloom Anglers, Well, another year of Ever-Bloom Tournament angling is fast approaching so I thought I would quit working on tackle and let you know of some changes that will take place this year. First, the Terminator Heavy Weight of the Year has changed to Bass Fan Army as the sponsor. The two anglers with the largest cumulative weight will win two spots in Bass Fan's Weekend Warrior Classic. This classic is highly regarded and one angler will win $25,000 this year. For more information on this event, just log on to http://www.bassfan.com. Terry will give more details on this at the first event. Second, we are pleased to announce that we have a full field of 25 boats signed up for the first event and a waiting list to get in. Because of this interest we are requesting that each team submit payment for the next tournament a minimum of 2 weeks before the event date. This will insure that we try and maintain a full field for eah event and can be handled in one of two ways: 1) Pay after the weigh-ins at the previous tournament to me only, or 2) Stop by Terry's office and pay him. Please remember it is cash only. We will not accept prepayment for any future dates. Lastly, we will now be paying cash for third place. That means money for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place finishers, Setyr rods for 4th place, and Strike King Top to Bottom Bags for 5th place. Again, please remember these sponsors when purchasing gear and tackle. In closing, we are excited about this year and the prospects it brings and hope you are too! We look forward to seeing you on the 2007 Ever-Bloom Tourney Trail. Remember to check the web site at https://everbloom.us for schedules and results. Mike Blake and Terry Brown.
