Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Jul 25 2009 5:00 AM - Sat, Jul 25 2009 1:00 PM
Location
Lake Bloomington

Flickr photos from the July 25, 2009 album.

The Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail visited Lake Bloomington on July 25 greeted by a cold front and wind, however the lake produced great fishing and 54 keepers were brought to the scales weighing 130.50 pounds. WOW! Starting at 5:15 am jackets were a must but temps later in the day rose to near 80 and fishing was even hotter. This lake is no longer Lake Doomington. The team of Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris bested the 20 boat field with 4 bass weighing 14.07 anchored by a 5.36 pound largemouth. They fished docks and grass. We would imagine a Zoom Brush Hog played here as well. Great job guys. In second place was the ever steady Dusty Whitacre and Jeff Myers with 5 bass weighing 12.88 pounds. Third place and the 2 Setyr Rods were Veteran EBTT anglers Butch Ridgeway and Larry Hagar with 11.93 pounds and taking 4th was Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 10.85 pounds. Fish were caught on docks, weeds, points and seawalls during the day and most teams reported catching a ton of small fish. The Sam Leman Heavyweight EverBloom Points leaders are Terry Brown and Mike Blake at 46.38 pounds followed by Dusty Whitacre and Bryan Estes with 41.92 pounds. Third place team is Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris with 39.18 pounds. Brown and Blake also lead Sam Leman Big Bass for the year with a 6.13 pounder. The next event is on Evergreen Lake August 15. Note: We will go back to a 6 am-2 pm tournament day at this event.
