Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Apr 14 2012 6:00 AM - Sat, Apr 14 2012 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Bloomington

Flickr photos from the April 14, 2012 album.

Stormy, wet, and cool conditions met a full field of 25 boats at the first Sam Leman Ever-Bloom Tournament at Lake Bloomington April 14. Main lake fish seemed to dominate the field and last years Sam Leman Heavyweight and Classic winners Brad Norris and Jamie Maisenbacher got an early start on a repeat performance in 2012 with a winning 5 bass limit of bass weighing 14.45 pounds. In second place was veteran crankbait experts Dave Norris and Greg Bayles with 5 that weighted 12.81 pounds. Norris fished most of the day with a crankbait attached to his hand as result of a mishap early in the day. That is one tough guy! In third place was James Hurt and Adam Stark with another 5 bass limit weighing 11.78 pounds. In fourth place was brother team of Randy and Rob Hakes with 10.36 and Fred Myers and super newcomer Luke Wiechman with 9.63 pounds, respectively. Myers and Wiechman won a Zoom Baits and Rapala prize assortment for their 5th place showing. Most of the successful teams targeted main lake points and flats and the first 1/4 mile of bank in the creeks and pockets using crankbaits, jigs and soft jerkbaits. Great job Jamie and Brad. Important Note: Don't forget all teams who wish to fish May 14th tournament at Lake Evergreen must pre-pay at the Wired2Fish Offices, 2024 Eagle Rd.
