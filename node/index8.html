Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, May 12 2012 6:00 AM - Sat, May 12 2012 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Evergreen

Flickr photos from the May 12, 2012 album.

Hakes and Hakes Win at Evergreen! Cloudy skies, very little wind and fish in all stages of the spawn greeted competitors at Lake Evergreen May 12th. What set up as perfect conditions and high expectations turned just the opposite with only 26 total bass brought to the scales. Brothers Randy and Rob Hakes took home first at the second Sam Leman EverBloom event with 4 fish weighing 10.53 pounds. They are also leading the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year standings after two events with 20.89 pounds. In second place was Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 3 fish weighing 8.15 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date

Location
Lake Bloomington

Flickr photos from the April 14, 2012 album.

Stormy, wet, and cool conditions met a full field of 25 boats at the first Sam Leman Ever-Bloom Tournament at Lake Bloomington April 14. Main lake fish seemed to dominate the field and last years Sam Leman Heavyweight and Classic winners Brad Norris and Jamie Maisenbacher got an early start on a repeat performance in 2012 with a winning 5 bass limit of bass weighing 14.45 pounds. In second place was veteran crankbait experts Dave Norris and Greg Bayles with 5 that weighted 12.81 pounds.
By Terry Brown, 29 February, 2012

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Aug 27 2011 6:00 AM - Sat, Aug 27 2011 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Bloomington

Flickr photos from the August 27, 2011 album.

Terry Brown and Mike Blake won the last regular season tournament on the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail at Lake Bloomington Saturday August 27 with 5 fish weighing 16.34 pounds. Jerry Martoglio and Joe Snyder had a limit that weighed 15.68 pounds for second. In third, also with a limit of fish and winners of the Duckett Fishing Microguide fishing rods were Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris with 13.29 pounds. Fourth place was Dave Whalen and Ryan Robinson with 12.30 and rounding out the top 5 were AJ Bailey and Scott Bree with 9.03 pounds.

Anglers Face Tough Fishing on 7th Stop

Sam Leman Heavyweight Race Heats Up

Full moon, calm conditions and light cloud cover meant fishing was tough on Evergreen Lake on the 7th stop on the Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail July 13th. Brian Bounds and Jon Hodge brought the only limit to the scales with 5 fish weighing 10.60 to take home the victory. Terry Brown and Mike Blake took second with 2 bass weighing 8.86 pounds anchored by a 4.67 pounder.

Therien and Therien Rapala Ever-Bloom Winners Leading Sam Leman Heavyweight for Team of the Year

The Therien Brothers, Mike and Aaron, are on a roll and even with temps near 95 degrees and reports of tough fishing they brought in a 5 bass limit weighing 17.78 to win the Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament at Lake Bloomington this past weekend. (July 30). They currently hold a 9.32 pound lead in the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year Standings over Terry Brown and Mike Blake.
