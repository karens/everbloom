Martoglio and Anderson win SKEBTT Classic

Veteran anglers Jerry Martoglio and Steve Anderson took top honors in the October 4 and 5 Strike King Ever-Bloom Classic on Evergreen and Lake Bloomington respectively. They were the 8th different team to win an event in 2008. Parity is indeed alive and well at the SKEBTT. They weighed 6 bass of 13.29 lbs for the honor. They edged out Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris with 11.42 lbs and Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 10.84 lbs. Blake and Brown won two custom Setyr Froggin rods. In fourth place were Fred Myers and Ken Petersen with 10.58 lbs. The Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year winners were Fred Myers and Ken Petersen with 56.35 lbs. In second were Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 52.71 lbs. Petersen and Tom Anderson had big bass honors for the year with a first tournament beauty at Lake Bloomington weighing 7.39 lbs. The next years tentative schedule is available and will be posted very soon. Remember to keep priority entry, money must be in for the first tournament before March 15 at Wired2Fish Headquarters at 2024 Eagle Road B2, Normal, Illinois. We will begin taking money January 1, 2009. Thanks to all who contributed with help and assistance this year, in particular thanks to Dusty Whitacre and Bryan Estes for assisting with the weigh ins. We are also looking for volunteers, it pays zero, to help next year with set-up, check-in and miscellaneous dirty jobs. The volunteers chosen will be the 2009 Tournament Committee. Email Terry at TBrown@wired2fish.com if you are interested. We are looking forward to another great season in 2009.
