Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Aug 14 2010 5:00 AM - Sat, Aug 14 2010 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Bloomington

Flickr photos from the August 14, 2010 album.

The Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament trail got a "big show" on Saturday August 14 as Brian Bounds and John Warmbir smoked the field with 22.97 pounds of bass to take the next to last regular season event. They won by over 14 pounds! They had the only limit and also had big bass of the tournament with a 6.45 pounder. That big bass was also the Sam Leman Big Bass of the Year so far in 2010. This is the largest 5 bass limit in SKEBTT history besting Tim Fauth and Scott Anderson's Evergreen limit of 21.97 pounds in 2006. Congrats guys and what a day! In second place was Brad Norris and Jamie Masenbacher with 7.99 pounds followed by Brian Estes and Dusty Whitacre with 7.79 pounds. Terry Brown and Mike Blake take a small 1.85 pound lead in the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year over Bounds and Hodge going into the regular season final August 28. They had over a 19 pound lead going into this past weekends qualifier. Way to go Brian and John! No lead is safe when this group of anglers hit the water. This is going to be real interesting again and the Classic does count in the year end final result so hang on. The next stop of the SKEBTT will be at Lake Evergreen on August 28. We will again start at 6:00 am for that event out of the Comlara Boat Ramp.
