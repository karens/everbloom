Long and Devine Win Rapala EverBloom Qualifier

Ron Long and Al Devine were the last boat out at take out but at the end of the day weighed the most fish to take the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail Qualifier at Lake Evergreen June 25th. They weighed 5 bass at 15.06 pounds. In second place were Jamie Maisenmacher and Brad Norris with 5 bass weighing 12.83 pounds. In third were Brian Bounds and Jon Hodge with 11.24 pounds. Fred Myers had Sam Leman Big Bass for the event with a 5.06 pounder. The Sam Leman Heavyweight Leaders are Aaron Therian and Mike Therian with 40.52 pounds.

Good Weather...finally!

The Fish Gods finally smiled and we had a great weather day on Lake Bloomington for stop 4 of the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail June 11. Its about time! The team of Sam Wheeler and Michael McDowell won the event with 5 fish weighing 16.89 pounds. In second were Rob and Randy Hakes with 15.10 pounds. They also had big bass of 5.50 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, May 28 2011 6:00 AM - Sat, May 28 2011 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Evergreen

Flickr photos from the May 28, 2011 album.

Is Mother Nature is mad at the Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail presented by Sam Leman Automotive Group? Three events and three rainy days. This time however temperatures were a bit more tolerable but fishing was super tough. Anglers reported catching lots of fish but measurable bass were tough to find. Brother Team of Randy and Rob Hakes found 3 bass that weighed 10.68 for the victory. In second were the veteran team of Jerry Martoglio and Loren Peters with 4 bass weighing 8.19 pounds. In third were Mike and Aaron Therien with 7.50 pounds. They also had big bass of 5.16 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, May 14 2011 6:00 AM - Sat, May 14 2011 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Bloomington
Bad Weather Again! Seems like every Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament has Mother Nature's bad list. Cold temps, high north winds and over 2 inches of rain followed a 80 degree day presenting miserable fishing conditions for the second Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail event presented by Sam Leman Automotive Group at Lake Bloomington. No hill for a climber as the father and son team of Randy and Hunter Ferguson had a limit of bass weighing 12.92 to take home the victory. Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris, also with a limit of fish finished second with 11.54 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Apr 16 2011 6:00 AM - Sat, Apr 16 2011 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Evergreen

Flickr photos from the April 16, 2011 album.

Brutal conditions met anglers for the first Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament on Evergreen Lake for 2011. High winds, rain/snow and 38 degrees greeted 23 teams. This was without hesitation this was one of the most brutal days of fishing ever encountered in the 9 year Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail history. The Rapala Ever- Bloom Tournament Trail has the best anglers in Central Illinois and big fish was the rule on this super tough day.
By Karen Stevenson, 11 February, 2011

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Aug 28 2010 6:00 AM - Sat, Aug 28 2010 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Evergreen

Flickr photos from the August 28, 2010 album.

The last regular season event of the Strike King Ever-Bloom TT was held August 28. Super low water conditions with the lake dropping nearly 5 feet in the last two weeks took much of the vegetation out of the equation, but as always the teams in this trail rose to the occasion with two limits and two 12 pound sacks of bass were brought to the scales. Red hot Dave Whalen and Ryan Robinson won the event with 5 bass weighing 12.97 pounds. They were followed closely by another super hot team of Brian Bounds and John Hodge with 5 bass weighing 12.64 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Aug 14 2010 5:00 AM - Sat, Aug 14 2010 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Bloomington

Flickr photos from the August 14, 2010 album.

The Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament trail got a "big show" on Saturday August 14 as Brian Bounds and John Warmbir smoked the field with 22.97 pounds of bass to take the next to last regular season event. They won by over 14 pounds! They had the only limit and also had big bass of the tournament with a 6.45 pounder. That big bass was also the Sam Leman Big Bass of the Year so far in 2010. This is the largest 5 bass limit in SKEBTT history besting Tim Fauth and Scott Anderson's Evergreen limit of 21.97 pounds in 2006.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Jul 17 2010 5:30 AM - Sat, Jul 17 2010 1:30 PM
Location
Lake Evergreen

The Strike King EverBloom Tournament Trail event on Lake Evergreen was a tale of two days. The fishing day was very hot and humid with temperatures in the 90's. At 2 pm a major storm blew in with heavy rain and lightning but the anglers from the SKEBTT proved tougher than the weather.

Rain soaked weigh ins are not fun but just about every team braved the elements to stay and watch. Thanks to Pat Welch, Dusty Whitacre and Mike Blake for making sure we got the fish weighed in and back into the water. Tough conditions for sure.
