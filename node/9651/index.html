Martoglio and Anderson win big!

Jerry Martoglio and Steve Anderson made it two in a row at the Strike King EverBloom Classic this past weekend by winning by over 10 pounds. They caught all of their bass on a obsolete Terminator T2 spinnerbait fishing shallow water wood. They had 10 bass weighing 28.68 pounds for the win. In second were Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 6 bass weighing 17.45 pounds. They also had a big bass of 6.54 pounds. They caught their bass both shallow and midrange on Gene Larew Biffle Bugs and Spro Bronzeye Frogs. Brown and Blake won the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year Award with 83.24 pounds bettering Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris by a little over 6 pounds. The 6.54 pounder also was the Sam Leman Big Bass of the Year. In third was Maisenbacher and Norris with 15.44 pounds on seven bass. Butch Ridgeway and Larry Hagar had big bass on Day 1 with a 4.05 pounder at Lake Bloomington. Next year's schedule is complete and will be posted soon. Thanks to all of the teams who fished the SKEBTT this year and we look forward to having you back next year. You are truly the best anglers in Illinois.
