Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Apr 16 2011 6:00 AM - Sat, Apr 16 2011 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Evergreen

Flickr photos from the April 16, 2011 album.

Brutal conditions met anglers for the first Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament on Evergreen Lake for 2011. High winds, rain/snow and 38 degrees greeted 23 teams. This was without hesitation this was one of the most brutal days of fishing ever encountered in the 9 year Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail history. The Rapala Ever- Bloom Tournament Trail has the best anglers in Central Illinois and big fish was the rule on this super tough day. Terry Brown and Mike Blake flipped the mid portions of the lake and won the event with 4 fish weighing 12.92 pounds. Matt Rice and Rick McMahan were second with 2 fish weighing 9.77 pounds anchored by a 8.16 pounder. In third with another huge fish were Rod Wallace and Patrick Welch with 1 fish weighing an amazing 8.65 pounds. This was the Sam Leman Big Fish of the tournament. Mike Campbell and Larry Russell were 4th with 1 fish weighing 5.99 pounds. Our next event is May 14th at Lake Bloomington.
