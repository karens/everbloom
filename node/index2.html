By Terry Brown, 29 May, 2018

Notice about June 2 tournament

I just confirmed with the Evergreen staff there is indeed a Triathalon June 2 so we will be going out of the West access, off of White Oak Rd that day for the Sam Leman Everbloom Trail Tournament. There will be no fishing past the buoys at the swimming beach area until 9am. Once the last swimmer has left the water you may fish the six mile/Deer Island area of the lake. We will still take off at 5:30 am and will weigh in at the West access. Feel free to give me a call with any questions. 218 259-7845. Please pass this on to the other teams.
