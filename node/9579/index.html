Tournament for Life

By Karen Stevenson, 5 April, 2009
The following was provided by Shawn Kobel: Most of you know me, but for those who don't my name is Shawn Kobel. My wife, Vicki, and I are having the first ever Relay for Life - Open Bass Tournament and we were hoping you could help us spread the word. My wife is a 5 year cancer survivor and the proceeds will go to our Relay Team. This event is in support of the Relay for Life of McLean County and the American Cancer Society. Here are the tournament details:
  • Date: 6/20/09
  • Location: Lake Evergreen
  • Check-in: 5am
  • Tournament time: 6am - 2pm
  • Weigh-in: starting 2pm
  • Entry fee: $100
  • 50 Teams Max: Secured entry when money received. First come first serve
  • Shirts can be ordered.
The following payout is based off 50 boat field:
  • 1st $1000
  • 2nd $600
  • 3rd $400
  • 4th $300
  • 5th $200
  • 6th $100
  • 1st Big Bass $300
  • 2nd Big Bass $100
We are currently in the process of collecting raffle/door prizes and are working on food for the event. We would like to thank the Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail and Bass World Sports for there early support. For teams that are interested simply have them complete and mail the attached document to: Shawn Kobel, 3416 Prescher Pt, Bloomington, IL 61704. Check made payable to Shawn Kobel. Please add "Relay for Life" in the memo. For questions, I can be reached at 309-261-3193. Again, there is a 50 boat limit. The first 50 entries receved are secured a spot.
