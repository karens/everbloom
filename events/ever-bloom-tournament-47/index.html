Good Weather...finally!

The Fish Gods finally smiled and we had a great weather day on Lake Bloomington for stop 4 of the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail June 11. Its about time! The team of Sam Wheeler and Michael McDowell won the event with 5 fish weighing 16.89 pounds. In second were Rob and Randy Hakes with 15.10 pounds. They also had big bass of 5.50 pounds. In third were Butch Ridgeway and Larry Hagar with 14.16 pounds. Randy and Rob Hakes are leading the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year Standings after 4 events with 33.03 pounds, followed by Aaron and Mike Therian with 32.19 pounds and Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 28.03 pounds, respectively. Rod Wallace and Pat Welch still have the Sam Leman Big Bass with an 8.65 pound giant. The next event is June 25 at Lake Evergreen. Note: We will begin at 5:30 am and fish until 2:00 pm that day!
