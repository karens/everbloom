Hog Heaven at Lake Bloomington

Date
Sat, Apr 26 2008 6:00 AM - Sat, Apr 26 2008 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Bloomington

Flickr photos from the April 26, 2008 album.

Lake Bloomington stalwarts Dave Norris and Greg Bayles won the first Strike King Ever-Bloom tournament of the year at Lake Bloomington on April 26 with 3 bass weighing 10.62 lbs. They had 3 bass that was anchored by a 6.30 lber. They are leading the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year Championship after one event. In second place was Ken Petersen and Tom Anderson with two bass weighing 10.03 lbs. They had a big bass of.......get this......7.39 lbs. That is a big fish anywhere and it was caught on a crankbait. As the story goes.....the fish had a single treble hook in the fish and the bait fell out while being netted. Great job guys and now the chase is a tough one for the Sam Leman Big Fish of the Year. The Sam Leman Big Fish of the Year pays $500.00. In third place was verteran anglers Jack Menssen and Junior Nottenkamper with 2 bass at 5.59 lbs. They won two Setyr premium rods for the third place. In fourth place was Mike Schilkofski and Aaron Therian with 2 bass weighing 4.60 lbs. The Strike King Top to Bottom winners were Randy Ferguson and Larry Russell with 1 bass weighing 4.68 lbs. They won an assortment of Strike King top to bottom lures for their efforts. There were 18 bass brought to the scales weighing just over 53 lbs. The complete results and photos are posted on https://everbloom.us. The next event is May 10 and you must prepay before the event to guarantee your spot in the tournament. Note: The Illinois Natural History Survey will be at the next tournament taking blood samples and testing bass. Remember to show them your catch!
