Ever-Bloom Tournament

Date
Sat, Aug 27 2011 6:00 AM - Sat, Aug 27 2011 2:00 PM
Location
Lake Bloomington

Flickr photos from the August 27, 2011 album.

Terry Brown and Mike Blake won the last regular season tournament on the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail at Lake Bloomington Saturday August 27 with 5 fish weighing 16.34 pounds. Jerry Martoglio and Joe Snyder had a limit that weighed 15.68 pounds for second. In third, also with a limit of fish and winners of the Duckett Fishing Microguide fishing rods were Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris with 13.29 pounds. Fourth place was Dave Whalen and Ryan Robinson with 12.30 and rounding out the top 5 were AJ Bailey and Scott Bree with 9.03 pounds. Big Bass was caught by Dave Norris and Greg Bayles and weighed 6.04 pounds. Brown and Blake are currently leading the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year with 74.18 pounds followed by Mike and Aaron Therien with 68.47 and Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris with 61.25 pounds. Sam Leman Big bass for the year is still held by Rod Wallace and Pat Welch with a 8.65 pound bass. The Rapala EverBloom Classic is September 17 and 18. Day One will be at Lake Bloomington and Day Two will be at Lake Evergreen. Day one we will fish from 6 to 3 pm and day two we will fish from 6 to 2:30 pm. Our dinner and awards, for participants, will follow immediately at the Big Shelter on the far west size of Comlara picnic grounds. Thanks to everyone for participating.
