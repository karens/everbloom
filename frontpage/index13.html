Martoglio and Anderson win big!

Jerry Martoglio and Steve Anderson made it two in a row at the Strike King EverBloom Classic this past weekend by winning by over 10 pounds. They caught all of their bass on a obsolete Terminator T2 spinnerbait fishing shallow water wood. They had 10 bass weighing 28.68 pounds for the win.

In second were Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 6 bass weighing 17.45 pounds. They also had a big bass of 6.54 pounds. They caught their bass both shallow and midrange on Gene Larew Biffle Bugs and Spro Bronzeye Frogs.

Brown and Blake won the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year Award with 83.24 pounds bettering Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris by a little over 6 pounds. The 6.54 pounder also was the Sam Leman Big Bass of the Year.

Lake Bloomington Shows its True Colors...

The last regular season Strike King EverBloom event on Lake Bloomington August 29 surprised the pundits. Cool temps, brisk northwest winds and intermittent clouds and sun in post frontal conditions usually spell a tough bite. Not so for the best anglers in Central Illinois as 3 limits and 36 bass weighing 97.05 pounds were brought to the scales. Super job guys!

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Mike Blake on Sun, 08/16/2009 - 6:44pm
Date: 
Saturday, August 15, 2009 -
6:00am to 2:00pm
Location: 
Lake Evergreen
Source: Flickr

Hot temps, brisk southwest winds and sunny conditions met 18 teams at the 7th stop on the Strike King EverBloom Tournament Trail at Lake Evergreen.

Rod Wallace and Pat Welch took home first place with a 5 bass limit weighing a very respectable 14.26 pounds. They flipped and pitched and caught their bass on an unnamed tube. Great job guys.

In second were veteran anglers AJ Bailey and Scott Bree with a 5 bass limit weighing 12.57 pounds. They too flipped and pitched to catch their bass.

In third place were Larry Russell and Randy Ferguson with two bass weighing 8.21 lbs anchored by the Sam Leman Big Bass of 5.15 pounds.

Rounding out the top 5 were Brad Norris and Jamie Maisenbacher with 6.86 pounds and Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 6.03 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Terry Brown on Mon, 07/27/2009 - 11:50am
Date: 
Saturday, July 25, 2009 -
5:00am to 1:00pm
Location: 
Lake Bloomington
Source: Flickr

The Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail visited Lake Bloomington on July 25 greeted by a cold front and wind, however the lake produced great fishing and 54 keepers were brought to the scales weighing 130.50 pounds. WOW! Starting at 5:15 am jackets were a must but temps later in the day rose to near 80 and fishing was even hotter. This lake is no longer Lake Doomington.

The team of Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris bested the 20 boat field with 4 bass weighing 14.07 anchored by a 5.36 pound largemouth. They fished docks and grass. We would imagine a Zoom Brush Hog played here as well. Great job guys.

In second place was the ever steady Dusty Whitacre and Jeff Myers with 5 bass weighing 12.88 pounds.
