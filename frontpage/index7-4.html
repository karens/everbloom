Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Karen Stevenson on Sun, 06/27/2010 - 6:42pm
Date: 
Saturday, June 26, 2010 - 5:30am to 1:30pm
Location: 
Lake Bloomington
Source: Flickr

The 5th Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament was held June 26 at Lake Bloomington. It started out with high humidity, stormy conditions and overcast skies and the bass were ready. Several bass over 5 pounds were weighed in and it was a slugfest.

Terry Brown and Mike Blake made it two in a row with a 5 bass limit weighing 19.62 pounds anchored by a 5.44 big bass.

In second was the team of Brian Bounds and John Hodge with 5 bass weighing 18.86 pounds.

Third place was Brad Norris and Jamie Maisenbacher with 4 bass weighing 12.90 pounds. Dave Norris and Greg Bayles had big bass of the event with a 6.28 pound bass. They also got 4th place with 10.58 pounds.

There were 34 bass weighed in weighing 92 pounds for a 2.71 average.

The Strike King EverBloom Tournament Trail competed on June 12 at Evergreen Lake. Hot humid conditions with little wind greeted the anglers.

Terry Brown and Mike Blake won with 5 bass weighing 17.32 pounds. They also had big bass of the event with a 5.44 pounder. They currently lead the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year standings with 34.71. Dave Whalen and Ryan Robinson are in second with 32.56 pounds.

Whalen and Robinson win at Bloomington...

Full moon, no wind, a beautiful day with light boat traffic and Dave Whalen had the flu. You couldn't tell it though as he and his partner Ryan Robinson walked away with the 3rd stop on the EverBloom Tournament Trail on Lake Bloomington with a 5 bass limit weighing 14.13. Whalen and Flynn have been on a tear recently and even the flu and a hat on sideways couldn't keep them from taking this event and also taking over the lead for the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year. They have 21.70 pounds after this event. They are followed by Ken Petersen and Fred Myers with 20.61 and Terry Brown and Mike Blake with

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Mike Blake on Sun, 05/16/2010 - 6:32am
Date: 
Saturday, May 15, 2010 - 6:00am to 2:00pm
Location: 
Lake Evergreen
Source: Flickr

The second Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail event was held on Evergreen Lake May 15. Two anomolies occurred.....1. Weather forecasts called for zero percent chance of rain. It rained cats and dogs and rain soaked anglers were looking for a weatherman to punch in the nose, and 2. A single day "boat on the lake" record was also broken with more boats than you can shake a stick at. This number should help the park keep up with budget reductions. The poor crappie were outnumbered.

Tough fishing was the norm and only 13 bass were weighed but Brian McDaniel and Steve Parks found 4 bass weighing 12.70 to take the victory. They were followed closely by Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 4 weighing 12.12 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Terry Brown on Sun, 04/25/2010 - 6:42am
Date: 
Saturday, April 17, 2010 - 6:00am to 2:00pm
Location: 
Lake Bloomington
Source: Flickr

The first event of the Strike King EverBloom Tournament Trail is in the books. Cold front conditions following an awesome week, weather wise, caused a tough bite and no limits were brought to the scales. Expectations were high but the results were weak in the opener.

Perrenial hammers on Lake Bloomington, Fred Myers and Ken Petersen, brought two fish to the scales weighing 7.51 pounds to seal the victory. As usual, they told us they caught them on jigs and chatterbaits. Might be a good idea to come up with another story guys for the next event. Their big bass weighed 4.12 pounds.
