Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Terry Brown on Mon, 08/16/2010 - 3:46am
Date: 
Saturday, August 14, 2010 - 5:00am to 2:00pm
Location: 
Lake Bloomington
Source: Flickr

The Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament trail got a "big show" on Saturday August 14 as Brian Bounds and John Warmbir smoked the field with 22.97 pounds of bass to take the next to last regular season event. They won by over 14 pounds! They had the only limit and also had big bass of the tournament with a 6.45 pounder. That big bass was also the Sam Leman Big Bass of the Year so far in 2010. This is the largest 5 bass limit in SKEBTT history besting Tim Fauth and Scott Anderson's Evergreen limit of 21.97 pounds in 2006. Congrats guys and what a day!

In second place was Brad Norris and Jamie Masenbacher with 7.99 pounds followed by Brian Estes and Dusty Whitacre with 7.79 pounds.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Terry Brown on Fri, 07/16/2010 - 11:12am
Date: 
Saturday, July 17, 2010 - 5:30am to 1:30pm
Location: 
Lake Evergreen

The Strike King EverBloom Tournament Trail event on Lake Evergreen was a tale of two days. The fishing day was very hot and humid with temperatures in the 90's. At 2 pm a major storm blew in with heavy rain and lightning but the anglers from the SKEBTT proved tougher than the weather.

Rain soaked weigh ins are not fun but just about every team braved the elements to stay and watch. Thanks to Pat Welch, Dusty Whitacre and Mike Blake for making sure we got the fish weighed in and back into the water. Tough conditions for sure.

Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Karen Stevenson on Sun, 06/27/2010 - 6:42pm
Date: 
Saturday, June 26, 2010 - 5:30am to 1:30pm
Location: 
Lake Bloomington
Source: Flickr

The 5th Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament was held June 26 at Lake Bloomington. It started out with high humidity, stormy conditions and overcast skies and the bass were ready. Several bass over 5 pounds were weighed in and it was a slugfest.

Terry Brown and Mike Blake made it two in a row with a 5 bass limit weighing 19.62 pounds anchored by a 5.44 big bass.

In second was the team of Brian Bounds and John Hodge with 5 bass weighing 18.86 pounds.

Third place was Brad Norris and Jamie Maisenbacher with 4 bass weighing 12.90 pounds. Dave Norris and Greg Bayles had big bass of the event with a 6.28 pound bass. They also got 4th place with 10.58 pounds.

There were 34 bass weighed in weighing 92 pounds for a 2.71 average.
