Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Terry Brown on Mon, 08/29/2011 - 9:25am
Date: 
Saturday, August 27, 2011 - 6:00am to 2:00pm
Location: 
Lake Bloomington
Source: Flickr

Terry Brown and Mike Blake won the last regular season tournament on the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail at Lake Bloomington Saturday August 27 with 5 fish weighing 16.34 pounds. Jerry Martoglio and Joe Snyder had a limit that weighed 15.68 pounds for second. In third, also with a limit of fish and winners of the Duckett Fishing Microguide fishing rods were Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris with 13.29 pounds. Fourth place was Dave Whalen and Ryan Robinson with 12.30 and rounding out the top 5 were AJ Bailey and Scott Bree with 9.03 pounds. Big Bass was caught by Dave Norris and Greg Bayles and weighed 6.04 pounds.

Anglers Face Tough Fishing on 7th Stop

Sam Leman Heavyweight Race Heats Up

Full moon, calm conditions and light cloud cover meant fishing was tough on Evergreen Lake on the 7th stop on the Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail July 13th.

Brian Bounds and Jon Hodge brought the only limit to the scales with 5 fish weighing 10.60 to take home the victory. Terry Brown and Mike Blake took second with 2 bass weighing 8.86 pounds anchored by a 4.67 pounder. The third place winners of the Duckett Fishing Microguide Rods were Michael McDowell and Sam Wheeler with 8.53 pounds..

The Sam Leman Heavyweight race tightened up with Aaron and Mike Therien still in the lead with 60.52 pounds followed by Blake and Brown with 57.84 and Bounds and Hodge with 57.24.
