Finally...Nice Weather

Stop 3 on the Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament trail was on Lake Bloomington May 27. Unbelievably great weather greeted 18 teams but no one told the bass they should be biting. Fish were in pre-spawn, spawn and post spawn and the bite was super tough. Only 12 keepers were brought to the scales but the cream indeed rose to the top again.

Bad Weather, Great Fishing Day

If you were Jamie Maisenbacher and his father Jim and you had over 18 lbs in the boat you would feel pretty good about winning round 2 of the Strike King Ever-Bloom Tournament trail on Evergreen Lake wouldn't you? No such luck, two other teams Tim Fauth and Scott Anderson and Fred Myers and Ken Peterson bested the Maisenbachers by over 3 lbs, with 21.92 and 21.59 respectively.

This was a real slugfest with the 3 winning weights each checking in one right after another. First Myers and Peterson, then Fauth and Anderson and finally the Maisenbachers who were one fish short of a 5 bass limit with 18.55 lbs.
