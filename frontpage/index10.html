Anglers Face Tough Fishing on 7th Stop

Sam Leman Heavyweight Race Heats Up

Full moon, calm conditions and light cloud cover meant fishing was tough on Evergreen Lake on the 7th stop on the Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail July 13th.

Brian Bounds and Jon Hodge brought the only limit to the scales with 5 fish weighing 10.60 to take home the victory. Terry Brown and Mike Blake took second with 2 bass weighing 8.86 pounds anchored by a 4.67 pounder. The third place winners of the Duckett Fishing Microguide Rods were Michael McDowell and Sam Wheeler with 8.53 pounds..

The Sam Leman Heavyweight race tightened up with Aaron and Mike Therien still in the lead with 60.52 pounds followed by Blake and Brown with 57.84 and Bounds and Hodge with 57.24.

Therien and Therien Rapala Ever-Bloom Winners Leading Sam Leman Heavyweight for Team of the Year

The Therien Brothers, Mike and Aaron, are on a roll and even with temps near 95 degrees and reports of tough fishing they brought in a 5 bass limit weighing 17.78 to win the Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament at Lake Bloomington this past weekend. (July 30). They currently hold a 9.32 pound lead in the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year Standings over Terry Brown and Mike Blake. Brown and Blake finished second in this event with 5 bass weighing 15.59 pounds followed closely by Brad Norris and Jamie Maisenbacher with 5 that weighed 15.19 pounds. Norris and Maisenbacher had big bass of 5.84 pounds.

Long and Devine Win Rapala EverBloom Qualifier

Ron Long and Al Devine were the last boat out at take out but at the end of the day weighed the most fish to take the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail Qualifier at Lake Evergreen June 25th. They weighed 5 bass at 15.06 pounds. In second place were Jamie Maisenmacher and Brad Norris with 5 bass weighing 12.83 pounds. In third were Brian Bounds and Jon Hodge with 11.24 pounds. Fred Myers had Sam Leman Big Bass for the event with a 5.06 pounder.

The Sam Leman Heavyweight Leaders are Aaron Therian and Mike Therian with 40.52 pounds. In second are Ryan Robinson and Dave Whalen with 34.20 pounds. Third is Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 33.39 pounds.

Good Weather...finally!

The Fish Gods finally smiled and we had a great weather day on Lake Bloomington for stop 4 of the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail June 11. Its about time!

The team of Sam Wheeler and Michael McDowell won the event with 5 fish weighing 16.89 pounds. In second were Rob and Randy Hakes with 15.10 pounds. They also had big bass of 5.50 pounds. In third were Butch Ridgeway and Larry Hagar with 14.16 pounds.

Randy and Rob Hakes are leading the Sam Leman Heavyweight of the Year Standings after 4 events with 33.03 pounds, followed by Aaron and Mike Therian with 32.19 pounds and Terry Brown and Mike Blake with 28.03 pounds, respectively. Rod Wallace and Pat Welch still have the Sam Leman Big Bass with an 8.65 pound giant.

Is Mother Nature is mad at the Rapala Ever-Bloom Tournament Trail presented by Sam Leman Automotive Group? Three events and three rainy days. This time however temperatures were a bit more tolerable but fishing was super tough. Anglers reported catching lots of fish but measurable bass were tough to find.

Brother Team of Randy and Rob Hakes found 3 bass that weighed 10.68 for the victory. In second were the veteran team of Jerry Martoglio and Loren Peters with 4 bass weighing 8.19 pounds. In third were Mike and Aaron Therien with 7.50 pounds. They also had big bass of 5.16 pounds. They won the two Duckett Fishing Microguide Rods. Scott Bree and AJ Bailey won the first out of the money Rapala Tackle package.
