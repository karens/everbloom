Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Terry Brown on Mon, 04/16/2012 - 10:31am
Date: 
Saturday, April 14, 2012 - 6:00am to 2:00pm
Location: 
Lake Bloomington
Source: Flickr

Stormy, wet, and cool conditions met a full field of 25 boats at the first Sam Leman Ever-Bloom Tournament at Lake Bloomington April 14.

Main lake fish seemed to dominate the field and last years Sam Leman Heavyweight and Classic winners Brad Norris and Jamie Maisenbacher got an early start on a repeat performance in 2012 with a winning 5 bass limit of bass weighing 14.45 pounds. In second place was veteran crankbait experts Dave Norris and Greg Bayles with 5 that weighted 12.81 pounds. Norris fished most of the day with a crankbait attached to his hand as result of a mishap early in the day. That is one tough guy!

Just Around the Corner!

Submitted by Terry Brown on Wed, 02/29/2012 - 6:55am

The Sam Leman EverBloom Tournament Trail, presented by Zoom Bait Company, is just around the corner and its time for those who fished with us last year to get your entries in. You have first chance as a prior angling team. All entries have to be received in the Wired2Fish offices by March 15 as a priority entry. New angler team entries will be accepted after that. Ever-Bloom Tournament

Submitted by Terry Brown on Mon, 08/29/2011 - 9:25am
Date: 
Saturday, August 27, 2011 - 6:00am to 2:00pm
Location: 
Lake Bloomington
Source: Flickr

Terry Brown and Mike Blake won the last regular season tournament on the Rapala EverBloom Tournament Trail at Lake Bloomington Saturday August 27 with 5 fish weighing 16.34 pounds. Jerry Martoglio and Joe Snyder had a limit that weighed 15.68 pounds for second. In third, also with a limit of fish and winners of the Duckett Fishing Microguide fishing rods were Jamie Maisenbacher and Brad Norris with 13.29 pounds. Fourth place was Dave Whalen and Ryan Robinson with 12.30 and rounding out the top 5 were AJ Bailey and Scott Bree with 9.03 pounds. Big Bass was caught by Dave Norris and Greg Bayles and weighed 6.04 pounds.
