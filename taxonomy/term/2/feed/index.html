field__item"><h3>Wegner and Geheber's Consistency wins Classic!</h3> You have heard about a long tailed cat in a room full of rocking chairs right?......He's a nervous feline. No question Andy Wegner was that cat at this year's Strike King Ever-Bloom TT Classic.
